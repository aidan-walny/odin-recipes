Street Corn Dip

Description:

Looking for a easy meal to please the crowd at your next gathering? We have you covered, with a simple, yet flavorful dip!

Ingredients

  • 16oz Cream Cheese
  • 2 Cans Southwest Corn
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 8oz Pepper Jack Cheese
  • 2/3 Cup Sour Cream
  • 5oz Cojita Cheese
  • Cilantro
  • Salt & Pepper, To taste
  • Paprika, To taste
  • Vaentina Salsa, To taste

Steps

  1. In a crockpot, add together all the ingredients listed above.
  2. Set the crockpot on high for 2 hours.
  3. Stir the mixture 30 minutes it's before it's done.
  4. Serve with your favorite chips!
